Munroe Score

This site is based on the XKCD comic, designed to put to the test the idea that every Wikipedia article eventually leads to Philosophy. This is now known as Munroe's Law.

The Munroe Score is the number of articles you have to go through before reaching Philosophy.

For more details on this phenomenon, check out this relevant wikipedia page.
